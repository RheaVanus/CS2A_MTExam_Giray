Calle Crisologo

Vigan City, Ilocos Sur

Calle Crisologo in Vigan is a historic cobblestone street lined with preserved Spanish-era houses. It gives visitors a glimpse of the Philippines’ colonial past. At night, the street glows beautifully with lanterns, offering a romantic atmosphere.

#CalleCrisologo #ViganHeritage #IlocosSur #TravelBackInTime #HistoricVigan

Bantay Bell Tower

Bantay, Ilocos Sur

Bantay Bell Tower is a centuries-old watchtower that once served as a lookout for pirates. Today, it offers panoramic views of Vigan and the surrounding countryside. It is a symbol of strength and resilience that attracts many tourists.

#BantayBellTower #ViganView #IlocosHistory #TravelIlocos #HeritageSpot

Pinsal Falls

Pinsal, Ilocos Sur

Pinsal Falls, the largest waterfall in Ilocos Sur, cascades majestically into a wide natural basin perfect for swimming. Surrounded by lush greenery and limestone formations, it offers both adventure and relaxation. Local legends and scenic views make it a must-visit for nature lovers and history buffs alike.

#PinsalFalls #IlocosSurAdventures #NatureEscape #HiddenGemPH #TravelIlocos
